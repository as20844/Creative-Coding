Scientific Name:

Elexia Bloblamorphus

Discovered by / at:

It was discovered by police, scientists did study on it.

Family:

The Elixer Blob is one singular creature, but it has a species surrounding it at all times, mainly for protection.

Habitat:

Its main habitat is in the forest, but it is currently in jail after it's been recognized as too much of a threat.

Appearance:

While it looks calm and non-threatening, even the slightest interaction will make this creature get angry, and cause it to perform an attack and get even larger.

Key Characteristics:

  • Turns green eventually.
  • Doesn't like human intereraction.
  • Attacks with purple balls based on interaction.
  • Flows in a unique way

What we know so far about Elixer Blob? What we know about this creature is that it was found in a forest, it was only small at the time. But when people started interacting with it, it started to get significantly bigger and caused a threat to others because it attacks with balls surrounding it. Police were called, and this creature was put into a jail cell.
Guidelines for human interaction with this creature How to interact with this creature is holding or pressing the mouse, in which you will see the creature getting bigger and more fierce, it unleashing the attack of balls. Be careful and don't hold down the mouse too long, or it will go insane...
